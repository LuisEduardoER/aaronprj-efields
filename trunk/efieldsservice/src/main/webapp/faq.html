Q: What if my order is unavailable?
A: If for some reason one or more of the items you order is not available, you will be given a full refund via the payment method you used to complete the order. If your order contains multiple items, they may be shipped to you individually as they arrive at The Book Abyss Online Bookstore. You will not incur additional shipping costs if this happens and we will be in constant communication with you to ensure you are kept informed of the progress of your order.
Back

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Once you have selected an item you wish to purchase, simply click on the "ADD TO CART" tab. You will then be automatically directed to a screen showing the details of your order.
Back

Q: How do I remove an item from the shopping cart?
A: To remove an item from your cart click on the red "X" button next to that item. To change the quantity of an item, enter the new quantity in the box next to that item, and select "UPDATE TOTALS".
Back

Q: How safe is it to use my credit card online?
A: All credit card transactions at M-Books online Bookstore are protected. Shopping online has never been safer.

Q: What are 'New Release' titles?
A: New release' denotes titles that have a release date in either the current month or, in some circumstances, the coming months. If your order contains 'new release' titles that are yet to be published, the book/s will be placed on back order and dispatched to you as soon as it becomes available. Once again, we will notify you imediately to inform you of the estimated availability date. Your credit card will not be charged for new release titles until they become available and are dispatched.TALS".
